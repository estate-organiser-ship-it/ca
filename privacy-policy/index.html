The Estate Organiser is a document you download and fill in on your own computer, device, or printed copy. The details you enter into it, your accounts, your passwords, your wishes, and everything else, stay entirely with you. We never see them, never receive them, never collect them, and never store them. We have no access to your completed book and no way to read what is inside it. It lives only where you choose to keep it.
The only information we hold is what you give us directly to set up your account and place your order: your name, your email, and your payment details (handled securely by Stripe and Systeme.io). Your estate information itself is never part of that. 1. Introduction This Privacy Policy explains how Prestige Worldwright Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our"), trading as The Estate Organiser, collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, sign up for our free resources, or purchase our products. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Australian Privacy Principles set out in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). 2. What information we collect We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:
  • Your name and email address (when you sign up for free resources, our newsletter, or purchase a product)
  • Payment information (processed securely through Systeme.io and Stripe, we do not store full payment card details ourselves)
  • Information you send us when you contact us by email
  • Technical data such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited
We also collect information automatically through cookies and tracking technologies, see Section 6. 3. How we use your information We use your personal information to:
  • Deliver the free resources, products, and courses you request
  • Process payments and provide customer support
  • Send you product updates, related content, and marketing communications (you can opt out at any time)
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Comply with our legal obligations
4. How we share your information We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only with trusted service providers who help us run our business, including:
  • Systeme.io, our website, course hosting, and email platform
  • Stripe, for payment processing

These providers are bound by their own privacy obligations and only access your data to provide services to us. We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect our legal rights. 5. By using our website and providing your information, you consent to your data being transferred to and stored in countries outside Australia. We take reasonable steps to ensure these providers handle your data in line with the Australian Privacy Principles. 6. Cookies and tracking We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website, including:
  • Essential cookies, required for the website to function

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not work properly. 7. How long we keep your information We retain your personal information for as long as you remain a customer or subscriber, and for a reasonable period afterwards to comply with legal and business obligations. 8. Your rights Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Request that we correct any inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us
9. How we protect your information We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online platform is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. 10. Children Our products and services are not directed at children under 18.
