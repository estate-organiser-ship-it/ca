Questions

Good to know

How long does it take to fill in?

Most people work through it over a few weeks, not a weekend, one section at a time, when you've got the energy. There's no rush, and you can save and come back any time.

Is my information safe?

Yes. It's a file you fill in on your own computer, or print and fill in by hand. Nothing is stored online or in anyone's cloud, so there's nothing to be breached. Where you keep it is entirely up to you.

Is this legal or financial advice?

No, and that's by design. The Estate Organiser is a place to record your information and wishes. It isn't a Will, and it doesn't replace your lawyer or accountant. It simply makes sure that when your family needs those professionals, they can find everything fast. Rules vary by province, so confirm the specifics with your own advisors.

Does it work in my province?

Yes. It's about gathering your accounts, passwords, documents and wishes in one place, which matters everywhere. Where province-specific steps come up, it points you to the right professional rather than guessing for you.

What exactly do I get?

Three files, delivered instantly: the digital fillable PDF, a printable edition, and the individual chapters so you can print just what you need. Updates are yours to keep.
